Frequently Asked Questions

Wisdora is known for eLearning. Why should I trust you with performance marketing?
Fair question. Our background in corporate training gave us deep expertise in two things most agencies lack: understanding audiences at a granular level, and creating content that actually engages people. Performance marketing is where these skills meet paid media. Our team includes dedicated ad specialists, and our creative team produces the ad assets in-house — no outsourcing, no delays, no disconnect between who writes the ad and who runs it.
How much does it cost?
Our management fees depend on your ad spend and campaign complexity. Most engagements start between ₹50,000–₹2L/month for management, with ad spend separate and fully controlled by you. We’ll give you a clear proposal after our strategy call.
How is Wisdora different from other performance marketing agencies?
Three things: (1) We have a 100+ person team that handles creative, landing pages, and ad management under one roof — no outsourcing gaps. (2) We offer month-to-month contracts with a performance commitment, not 12-month lock-ins. (3) We optimize campaigns daily, not weekly. You see every change in a live dashboard.
How soon will I see results?
Most clients see their first qualified leads within 7–14 days of campaign launch. Meaningful, profitable scaling typically happens within 60–90 days as we optimize based on real conversion data.
What platforms do you manage?
Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Performance Max, Display, YouTube) and Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram). These two platforms drive 90%+ of performance marketing results for most businesses.
Will I own my ad accounts and data?
Absolutely. We run campaigns from YOUR ad accounts. You own all data, audiences, creatives, and campaign history. If we ever part ways, everything stays with you.
What does the process look like to get started?
Fill out the contact form. We’ll schedule a 30-minute strategy call within 24 hours. If we’re a good fit, we deliver a custom campaign blueprint within 5 business days. You approve, we launch. Simple.
